# Mission Control Market Insights

## Habit: Weekly scan
We’ll treat market research as an ongoing habit—digest 1–2 relevant articles or reports each week and log the takeaways here so the product narrative stays grounded in real demand.

### 1. DevOps dashboards as mission control (Apr 16, 2025)
- Source: CloudZero “DevOps Dashboard Ultimate Guide”
- Takeaways: Teams expect real-time visibility, accountability, and a single pane that integrates dev, ops, and cost data. Customizable role-based views and tool integrations make dashboards indispensable for high-performing DevOps.

### 2. Product Ops dashboards spotlight operational rigor (Jul 2025)
- Source: ProductPlan “How to Create a Product Operations Dashboard in 4 Steps”
- Takeaways: Product ops adds routine, metrics, and experiment tracking; dashboards are the shared scorecard that keeps stakeholders aligned on KPIs and experiments. Mission Control can mimic this by surfacing curated KPIs + live experiments (e.g., heartbeat results).

### 3. Automation market is accelerating (2024–2033 forecast)
- Source: PRNewswire / Market Research Intellect (Data Warehouse Automation Market)
- Takeaways: The automation software market (cloud/AI/data pipeline) is expected to grow from $3.5B in 2024 to $10.2B by 2033 (CAGR 15.7%), driven by demand for faster integrations and fewer manual processes. Provides credibility for pitching Mission Control as automation + dashboard product.
